Cloud Guide

The Cloud Guide is a universal tool to help guide you through basic crowdfunding and BlueCloud Principles. It is a wonderful go-to handbook for any questions, concerns, or relevant information on what we offer and what you should be made aware of! The content herein represents BlueCloud’s viewpoints on how our site should be conducted and what we believe will help you as a user make better choices, stay informed, and think differently. This Guide should also serve as the way you conduct your own actions and behaviors through the use of BlueCloud. This is a professional, entrepreneurial-driven environment and we do our best to nurture our community atmosphere as appropriately and accordingly as possible. The Cloud Guide has been set up to be as transparent as possible for everyone to see and recognize the culture we hope to spread as BlueCloud.
